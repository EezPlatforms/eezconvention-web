Why it exists

eezconvention covers the work that starts after a convention is announced.

The mobile app already models the real workflow: admins create conventions, attendees register, committees choose how they enter, navigate schedule and agenda flows, committees coordinate tasks, finance reviews spending, and staff track receipts through completion. This website is the front door for that platform.

Core Capabilities

A convention control center for organizers and attendees.

Conventions

Create and register for conventions

Organizers can create branded conventions with logos, dates, descriptions, and location data. Attendees can search nearby conventions by ZIP code, register directly, and enter the right convention from a dedicated convention picker.

Attendees

Support the full attendee flow

After registration, attendees can continue as attendees for a convention, access a QR pass, browse the event schedule, build a personal agenda, and jump into attendee-specific experiences from one place.

Committees

Run committees with clear ownership

Admins manage committees, roles, and membership. Chairs and co-chairs can add members and keep work routed to the right team.

Requests

Track operational requests end to end

Standard requests move through submitted, assigned, in-progress, completed, closed, and reopened states with filters for personal and team views.

Budget

Route budget approvals the right way

Budget requests include amount, vendor, committee, payment method, needed-by date, and justification, then route directly to Budget and Finance.

Expenses

Capture real spending with documentation

Teams can record expenses, attach invoices and receipts, and keep finance records tied to the exact committee and convention.

Visibility

See convention health at a glance

Dashboard summaries surface open tasks, overdue items, review queues, committee load, and budget signals so leaders can act before things stall.

Explore

Give attendees useful tools during the event

The attendee explore flow includes seminar signup and waitlist support, booth directory and map access, announcements, and attendee feedback screens.

Attendee Flow

Not just registration. The attendee journey continues inside the app.

The mobile app supports an explicit attendee persona. Users can choose a convention, continue as an attendee, and move through attendee-first tabs and tools instead of being dropped into committee operations.

That means the product handles both sides of a convention: the internal execution layer for organizers and the navigational experience for attendees during the event itself.

A1

Find and register

Search conventions by ZIP code and register directly from the app.

A2

Choose convention and persona

Select a convention from My Conventions and continue as an attendee when entering.

A3

Use attendee tabs

Open Event Schedule, My Agenda, Explore, and QR Pass / Check-in from the attendee experience.

A4

Explore on-site features

Access seminar signup, booth directory and map, announcements, and feedback tools during the event.
